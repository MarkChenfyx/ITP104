Final Project Summary

Topic & Purpose

This website is a personal academic portfolio for Baicheng Chen (Mark), a USC freshman majoring in Mathematics and Philosophy. The goal of the site is to introduce myself to professors, recruiters, and peers through a simple, professional online presence.

Pages

  • index.html – Landing page with a short welcome and photo
  • about.html – Introduction to my background, interests, and academic direction
  • resume.html – Resume page with downloadable PDF
  • gallery.html – A visual page featuring personal photos and music
  • contact.html – Contact form that links to a confirmation page
  • confirmation.html – Simple confirmation page
  • summary.html – This summary document

Design

The site uses HTML/CSS layout following lectures. The color scheme is based on soft neutrals. The design is minimalistic, with attention to readability and consistency across all pages.

Extras Included

  • Form – A contact form that submits to a confirmation page
  • Responsive Design – Media queries for mobile layout
  • Audio – Background music added in the gallery
  • Transition – Smooth hover color transitions in navigation

Plugin Use

No external JavaScript or jQuery plugins are used in this project.
